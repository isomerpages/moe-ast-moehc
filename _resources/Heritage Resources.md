##### **Research Resources: Useful links**
<img src="/images/heritageresources8.jpg" style="width:30%;margin-right:15px;" align="left">
<b>Here are some useful websites and portals for doing content research:</b>

NLB's Resource Guide on&nbsp;**[Development of Public Education in Singapore](http://www.nlb.gov.sg/resourceguides/development-of-education-in-singapore/)**
  
NLB's&nbsp;**[OneSearch Portal](https://search.nlb.gov.sg/)**&nbsp;to search across all NLB, NHB and National Gallery resources.  
  
National Archives of Singapore's&nbsp;**[Online Search Portal](https://www.nas.gov.sg/archivesonline/)**&nbsp;for old photographs, oral history, audio &amp; visual recordings etc.  
  
NHB's&nbsp;**[Roots](https://www.roots.sg/)**&nbsp;portal for the National Collection and other NHB resources.
