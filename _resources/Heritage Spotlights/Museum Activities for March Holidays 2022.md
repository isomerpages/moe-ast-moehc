### **Museum Activities for March Holidays 2022**

<img src="/images/marchhol1.png" style="width:85%">

11 Mar 2022 I 2 min read<br>
The March School Holidays are here! If you are wondering what to do this week, we have put together for you some family activities that are happening at various museums and galleries! Check them out below:

1\. March Holidays Family Fun by the **[National Museum of Singapore](https://www.nhb.gov.sg/nationalmuseum/our-programmes/programmes-list/march-ff-22?sc_lang=en)**

<img src="/images/marchhol2.jpeg" style="width:50%" align=left>

<br clear="left">

Check out the whole range of on-site and online programmes offered by the National Museum, including two drop-in activity spaces, where families can take a closer look at inter-generational artworks and exchanges, sparking your own family conversations.

2\. March On by the **[Esplanade](https://www.esplanade.com/festivals-and-series/march-on/2022)**

<img src="/images/marchhol3.jpg" style="width:50%" align=left>

<br clear="left">

Esplanade has a new annual children’s festival that explores relevant issues for the young through different experiences. In this edition, take part in various activities and performances that tackle the topic of bullying, and take this opportunity to spend time together and build stronger relationships with your family.

3\. Apa Khabair? Peranakan Museum in the Making** by **[Asian Civilisations Museum](https://www.nhb.gov.sg/acm/whats-on/exhibitions/apa-khabair)**

<img src="/images/marchhol4.jpeg" style="width:50%" align=left>

<br clear="left">

Did you know that the Peranakan Museum building used to be Taonan School? Learn more about the key milestones from the Peranakan Museum’s past and also the Peranakan way of life and culture from Singapore and the region in this pop-up exhibition!
