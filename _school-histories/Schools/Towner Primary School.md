#### **School History**
Located at McNair Road, Towner Primary School was established from the merger of Whampoa English School and Towner School in 1984.

In 1997, Towner Primary School was closed and in 1998, its school building made way for HDB flats. The schoolâ€™s remaining students joined May Primary School.
