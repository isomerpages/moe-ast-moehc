#### **School History**
Established in the mid to late 1960s, Upper Aljunied Technical School was located at Upper Aljunied Road. The school shared a field with neighbouring Sang Nila Utama Secondary School.  
  
In 1996, Upper Aljunied Technical School closed. The school building has since been used as a student dormitory.
