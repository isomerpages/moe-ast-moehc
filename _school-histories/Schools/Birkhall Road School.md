#### **School History**
Established in 1956, Birkhall Road School was located along Margaret Drive. The school was also known as Birkhall School and Birkhall Primary School.  
  
Birkhall Road School shared a field with neighbouring Strathmore Primary School and Queenstown Primary School. In 1984, Birkhall Primary School merged with Queenstown Primary School. Since then, Birkhall Primary Schoolâ€™s site has been used by Queenstown Primary School.
