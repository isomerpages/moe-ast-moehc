#### **School History**
Founded on 2nd February 1946, Nam San School students were housed in three separate locations: temple, wayang stage and warehouse.  

In 1947, Nam San School was relocated to a school building at Choa Chu Kang Road. In 1978, a new building for Nam San School was officially opened. 

In 1990, Nam San School merged with Ama Keng School to form South View Primary School. Since then, South View Primary School has been located at Choa Chu Kang Central.  

Principals:<br>
\- Mr Kua Chu Yan (1946 – 1971)<br>
\- Mr Chan Cheok Lock (1971 -  unknown)
