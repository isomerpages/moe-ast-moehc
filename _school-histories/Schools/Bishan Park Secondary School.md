#### **School History**
Bishan Park Secondary School was established on 2 January 1993 with 15 teachers, four support staff, and 443 students. The school operated temporarily at the premises of Whitley Secondary School before moving to its new campus on 28 June 1993. The school was officially opened on 5 July 1996.

The school merged with Peirce Secondary School in 2018 and has since been known as Peirce Secondary School, operating at the latter’s premises.

Principals:<br>
