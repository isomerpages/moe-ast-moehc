#### **School History**
Jin Shan Primary School was founded in 1982.  
  
In 1984, the school shifted to Clementi New Town and was officially opened in 1986.  
  
In 1997, the school ceased operation. Since 1998, Nan Hua Primary School has occupied the former site of Jin Shan Primary School.

Principals:
\- Mr Mohd. Amin Rasimon (1984)
