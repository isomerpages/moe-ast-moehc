#### **School History**
Mattar Primary School was established in 1968 along Mattar Road. The school was next to Mattar East School and Aljunied Primary School. All three schools shared a large field.&nbsp;

In 1988, Mattar Primary School merged with Mattar East School. In 1991, Mattar Primary School was closed. Students of Mattar Primary School were provided alternative places in MacPherson Primary School.
