#### **School History**
North Spring Primary School was established in 2000 as a government co-educational school on Rivervale Street.&nbsp;The Indoor Sports Hall and PERI-Upgrading of the school was completed in December 2014.&nbsp; The school started operating as a single-session school in 2014.&nbsp;&nbsp;In 2014, the school was accorded the Learning for Life Programme in Community Service and Student Leadership. The following year, the school was accorded a second Learning for Life Programme in Sports and Outdoor Education.&nbsp;The School-based Student Care Centre started operations in 2015. The project for Synthetic Turfing of the school field was completed in March 2016.&nbsp;

Principals:<br>
\- Mr Tay Kim Huat (2000–2004)<br>
\- Ms Shirley Ng Ah Luat (2005–2009)<br>
\- Mrs Angeline Loh (2010–present)
