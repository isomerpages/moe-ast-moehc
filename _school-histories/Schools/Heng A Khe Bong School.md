#### **School History**
Heng A Khe Bong School was an amalgamation of two Chinese primary schools in the vicinity of Telok Ayer - Heng A School, established in 1918 and Khe Bong School, established in 1920. &nbsp;&nbsp;It was established in 1971 when both schools merged and relocated to Toa Payoh New Town. It was officially opened in 1975.

In 1993, Heng A Khe Bong School closed.

Principals:
\- Mr Choo Kwee Wah (1979 - 1983)
