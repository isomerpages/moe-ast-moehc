#### **School History**
Xinghua Primary School was founded in 1930. The school was then known as Sing Hua Public School. In 1957, the school became a government-aided school. In 1973, the school became an integrated government school offering English and Chinese as first languages. In 1985, Sing Hua Public School was renamed Xinghua Primary School. In 1985, the school moved to its present location at 45 Hougang Avenue 1.

In 2003, Xinghua Primary School merged with Charlton School. In 2005, the school was officially declared open. In 2007, Xinghua Primary School merged with Parry Primary School.
