#### **School History**
Established in 1975, Membina Primary School was situated at the junction of Jalan Membina and Jalan Bukit Merah. It was the first school with a contemporary design, constructed under the Education Ministryâ€™s School Building Programme. It had 1817 pupils at the time of opening.&nbsp;&nbsp;

In 1990, Membina Primary School took in students from Tiong Bahru Primary School when the latter closed.&nbsp;

Membina Primary School was closed in 1996.
